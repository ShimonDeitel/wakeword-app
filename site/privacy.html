Privacy Policy

Wakeword is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage area. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, usage analytics, or identifiers. The app does not require an account and does not use any third-party tracking or advertising SDKs. In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple's App Store via StoreKit; we never see your payment information.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is removed with it.
